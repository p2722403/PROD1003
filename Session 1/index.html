Stacey Henson

Stacey Henson is a professional photographer, video producer, graphic designer and creative director based in London.

Over the years I have provided high quality work to clients for a variety of model shoots, music videos, advertisements, event photography, and graphic design. Choosing not to limit my work and creative horizen i've been able to work with a variety of clients. I'm always looking for new people to aspire and aim to help aid others creative ventures with my own love of the craft of creation.

"When it seems impossible to live in the moment, photography captures that moment for a lifetime"
